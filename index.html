Places to Visit

Maasai Mara

Maasai Mara (Masai Mara) is situated in south-west Kenya and is one of Africa’s Greatest Wildlife Reserves. Together with the Serengeti National Park in Tanzania it forms Africa’s most diverse, incredible and most spectacular eco-systems and possibly the world’s top safari big game viewing eco-system.

Hell's Gate

This is a place named for intense activity on its boundaries.Towering cliffs,stark rock towers, scrub clad volcanoes and belching plumes of geothermal steam are some of the spectacular scenery.

Kisumu Impala Sanctuary

The Kisumu Impala Sanctuary is destination to enjoy natural beauty that abounds here. Te sanctuary hosts a variety of wild animals like impalas,big cats,buffaloes and several primate species.The sanctuary is also home to five campsites all with spectacular views of Lake Victoria.
